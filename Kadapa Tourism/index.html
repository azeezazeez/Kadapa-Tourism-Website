Popular Destinations

Must-visit places in and around Kadapa

Gandikota

Known as the Grand Canyon of India, featuring a stunning gorge and ancient fort.

Vontimitta

Home to the magnificent Kodandarama Swamy Temple, a masterpiece of Vijayanagara architecture.

Ameen Peer Dargah

A revered Sufi shrine attracting devotees of all faiths, known for its annual Urs festival and spiritual significance.

Famous Events

Experience the vibrant culture and traditions of the region through these grand celebrations.

Vontimitta Brahmotsavam

A celestial wedding ceremony of Lord Rama and Sita, celebrated with immense devotion under the full moon.

Annual Celebration

Ameen Peer Urs

Annual festival celebrating the Sufi saint Ameen Peer, featuring qawwali, prayers, and a vibrant fair attracting thousands of devotees.
